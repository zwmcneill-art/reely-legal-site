Privacy Policy

We built Reely to help households watch better together. This policy explains clearly what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

Who We Are

Reely is a mobile app that helps households find movies and TV shows to watch together on their existing streaming services. References to “Reely,” “we,” “our,” and “us” in this policy refer to the Reely app and its operators.

Questions about this policy can be directed to reelysupport@gmail.com.

Data Collection

What We Collect and Store

We only collect information that is necessary to provide the Reely experience. Here is a plain-language breakdown of what is stored:

  • 👤 Account info — your email address, display name, and optionally a first name, last name, phone number, and a short bio. Email is required for authentication; all other fields are optional.
  • 📺 Streaming service selections — which services (e.g. Netflix, Hulu, Max) you indicate you subscribe to. This is used solely to filter recommendations to titles available on your services.
  • 🏠 Household preferences — settings such as content rating limits, family-friendly filtering, and content type preferences. These are used to personalise recommendations.
  • 👥 Viewer profiles — names, avatars, and taste preferences (favourite and disliked genres, preferred runtime, content type) for each household member you create. Viewer profiles are local to your account; they are not linked to or shared with any other user.
  • 📋 Watchlist — titles you have saved for later. Only you can see your watchlist.
  • 🕐 Watch history and reactions — when you mark a title as watched and optionally rate it (loved, liked, mixed, disliked, didn't finish), we store that record along with which viewer profiles were present. This is used to improve future recommendations and to show you your watching habits.
  • 🎯 Match session history — when you run a group session to find something to watch, we store the results shown, titles skipped, and the title your group chose (if you record one). This history is visible only to you.
  • 📊 Usage analytics — we collect anonymised, aggregated events (such as which screens are visited and which features are used) to understand how the app is performing and to improve it. See the Analytics section below for more detail.

Usage

How We Use Your Data

  • 🔍 Personalised recommendations — your viewer profiles, watch history, and reactions are used to rank and filter title suggestions during group sessions. This processing happens within the app and on our servers; your data is never shared with third-party advertising networks for this purpose.
  • 📈 Product improvement — aggregated, anonymised usage patterns help us understand what is working and what needs improvement. Individual watch history is not sold or shared for product improvement.
  • 🔒 Account security — your email and authentication credentials are used to identify you, keep your account secure, and enable password recovery.
  • ✉️ Support communication — if you contact us, we use your email address to respond. We do not initiate unsolicited marketing emails.
We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your watch history, viewer profiles, or personal information with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

Third-Party Data

Third-Party Content Providers

Title information, streaming availability, poster images, and related metadata displayed in Reely are sourced from third-party content databases and streaming platform data providers. We do not control the accuracy, completeness, or continued availability of this data.

When you tap a “Watch on [Service]” link, you may be directed to a third-party app or website. Once you leave Reely, that service’s own privacy policy and terms apply. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external services.

Content availability may change without notice. A title appearing in Reely search results does not guarantee it is currently available on your streaming service.

Analytics

Usage Analytics

Reely uses PostHog, a product analytics service, to understand how the app is used. We collect usage events such as which screens were visited, whether a session was started, and whether a recommendation was selected.

These events are linked to a unique account identifier so we can understand usage patterns across sessions. Basic account information — including your email address and display name — may be associated with your account profile in PostHog for attribution purposes. Individual event properties do not contain your personal information, and this data is not used for advertising.

PostHog's privacy policy is available at posthog.com/privacy.

Retention

Data Retention and Account Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we permanently remove the following from our servers:

  • 👤Your profile and account information
  • 📺Streaming service selections and household preferences
  • 👥All viewer profiles in your household
  • 📋Your watchlist
  • 🕐Watch history and reactions
  • 🎯Match session history
  • 🔑Your authentication credentials

Account deletion is initiated from within the app under Settings → Delete Account. You will be asked to confirm before any deletion occurs. Deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

Anonymised, aggregated analytics data that cannot be re-linked to your identity may be retained after account deletion for product analysis purposes.

Age

Children’s Privacy

Reely is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Updates

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of Reely after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
