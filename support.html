Guidance

How to Get Help

For the fastest resolution, follow these steps when contacting support:

1
Describe the problem clearly
Tell us what you were trying to do, what happened, and what you expected to happen. Screenshots are always helpful.
2
Include your app version
You can find the app version in Settings → Account Details → App version. This helps us diagnose version-specific issues.
3
Include your device type
Let us know your device model and operating system version (e.g. iPhone 15 Pro, iOS 18.1).
4
Send to reelysupport@gmail.com
Email us with the subject line starting with the issue type — for example: "[Bug] Session screen crashes" or "[Question] How do I delete a viewer?"

Common Topics

Frequently Asked Questions

📡
Streaming services show no results
Make sure your streaming services are selected in Settings → Streaming Services. If you see a loading spinner that never resolves, try closing and reopening the app.
👥
How do I add a viewer?
Go to the Viewers tab and tap the + Add button. Each viewer profile stores individual genre and content preferences.
🎯
Recommendations don’t match our taste
Make sure each viewer’s genre preferences are filled in under their profile. Mark watched titles with a reaction — your feedback helps shape future recommendations.
🔒
I can’t log in
If you’ve forgotten your password or can’t get in, email us and we’ll help you regain access.
🎬
A title says it’s on a service but it’s not
Streaming availability changes frequently. Content data comes from a third-party provider and may not reflect same-day changes. If you notice repeated errors, let us know.
📊
Viewing stats look wrong
Stats are calculated from your watch history. Make sure you’re marking titles as watched after sessions. Your viewing activity is summarised under Settings → Viewing Stats.

Bugs

Reporting a Problem

If you’ve found a bug — something that crashes, behaves unexpectedly, or looks broken — please send us a bug report. Include:

  • 📝 Steps to reproduce — exactly what you tapped or did before the issue occurred.
  • 📱 Device and OS version — e.g. iPhone 13 Mini, iOS 17.4.
  • 🔢 App version — found in Settings → Account Details → App version.
  • 🖼️ Screenshots or screen recording — if possible. A picture is worth a thousand words.

Send bug reports to reelysupport@gmail.com with the subject line starting with [Bug].

Account

Account Deletion Help

You can delete your account directly from within the Reely app:

  • 1️⃣ Open the app and go to Settings.
  • 2️⃣ Scroll to the Danger Zone section.
  • 3️⃣ Tap Delete Account.
  • 4️⃣ Review what will be deleted, type DELETE to confirm, and tap Delete My Account.

Deletion permanently removes your account and all associated data. This cannot be reversed. If you’re having trouble deleting your account from within the app, email us and we will assist you.

For a full list of what is deleted when you delete your account, see our Privacy Policy under “Data Retention and Account Deletion.”
